Site Name

Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce

A generic name was chosen based on the City of Idaho Falls, ID
Taken from the Idaho Falls website(www.idahofallsidaho.gov). "IF serves as a regional hub for health care, travel and business in southeast Idaho.
The community's economy was mostly agriculturally focused until the Atomic Energy Commission opened the
National Reactor Testing Station in the nearby desert in 1949."



Site Purpose

The main purpose of this website is to promote interest in local business possibilities

  • Make business contacts
  • Receive chamber newsletters
  • Obtain customer referrals
  • Bring credibility to local businesses
  • Increase local businesses visibility in the community
  • Develop Networking opportunities
